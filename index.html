Afghanistan’s cities are living cultural treasures that tell unique stories. From Balkh, with its long history and Buddhist influence, to Kabul, the country’s cultural and political center, each ancient city has its own unique architecture, customs, and handicrafts to portray. Despite many challenges, these cities have preserved a rich and diverse culture that is a testament to Afghanistan’s resilience and national identity.
